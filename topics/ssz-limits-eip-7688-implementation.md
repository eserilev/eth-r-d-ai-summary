# SSZ limits and EIP-7688 implementation

---

## 2026-05-08 (epbs)

Tersec clarified that SSZ limits are already addressed by EIP-7688, specifically through the converted types outlined in the EIP specification. The EIP has been confirmed for inclusion (CFI'd) in the Glamsterdam upgrade but hasn't been implemented yet because it's not considered a headline feature for the upgrade.

Tersec concluded that if the goal is to increase SSZ limits, this will happen automatically through the natural progression of EIP-7688's implementation in Glamsterdam, suggesting no additional action is needed to address the SSZ limits concern.
