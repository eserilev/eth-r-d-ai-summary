# ePBS Breakout Call Meeting Link Issues

**Channel:** epbs | **Date:** 2025-12-05

## Summary

The ePBS breakout call encountered technical difficulties when the original Zoom meeting link from the ethereum/pm agenda was missing the required password. Participants were unable to join the meeting using the initial link (https://zoom.us/j/86537060608), prompting immediate troubleshooting efforts from the organizers.

The issue was resolved by creating a replacement Zoom meeting with full credentials: Meeting ID 850 6282 9915 and Passcode 122490. The organizers suspected that the Zoom API may have caused the original link failure, leading them to spin up an entirely new meeting room. Both the GitHub agenda issue and calendar were updated with the corrected meeting information to ensure participants could access the call.
