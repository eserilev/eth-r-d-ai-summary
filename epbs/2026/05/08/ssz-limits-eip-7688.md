# SSZ Limits and EIP-7688

**Channel:** epbs | **Date:** 2026-05-08

## Summary

tersec clarified that SSZ limits are already addressed by EIP-7688, specifically pointing to the converted types section of the proposal. The EIP has been marked as "Considered for Inclusion" (CFI) for the Glamsterdam upgrade but hasn't been implemented yet since it's not considered a headline feature.

According to tersec, if the goal is to increase SSZ limits, this will happen automatically through the natural progression of EIP-7688's implementation in the upcoming upgrade cycle.
